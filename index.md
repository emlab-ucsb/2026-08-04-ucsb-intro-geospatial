Welcome to the Intro to Geospatial Raster and Vector data workshop for the Ocean Science Expedition Academy!

We have a fun workshop planned for you all. 

In addition to class time on **Tuesdays from 3-5p PT**, we will offer an optional "Office Hours" time on **Thursdays from 4-5p PT**. These Office Hours will alternate between troubleshooting sessions where you can bring anything in R that you've been working on and would like some guidance with, and R lectures, where scientists will come and give a presentation about how they have used R in their work. 

There will be a kick-off Office Hour on 2026-08-03 from 4-5p PT for help downloading R, RStudio, and the datasets required for this workshop.

We have the schedule for both below.
